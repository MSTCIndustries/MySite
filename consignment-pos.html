What's Included

Everything your shop needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Built from the ground up for consignment and resale operations. Every feature is designed around how consignment shops actually run day-to-day.

👤

Consignor Tracking

Manage every consignor with a dedicated profile — contact info, consignment history, active items, and payout balances all in one view.

📦

Item Intake

Fast intake flow for logging new items — assign to consignor, set price, categorize, and print tags without leaving the screen.

📊

Live Inventory

Real-time inventory view across all consignors and categories. See what's on floor, what's sold, and what needs to be returned.

💳

Sales Processing

Smooth checkout with barcode scanning, split tender, and automatic consignor split calculation at the point of sale.

💸

Automated Payouts

Generate payout reports per consignor automatically. Know exactly what you owe, when you owe it, without manual math.

📈

Reporting

Daily sales summaries, top-selling consignors, category performance, and payout history — exportable and easy to read.
